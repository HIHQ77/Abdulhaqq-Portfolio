Educational Background

My coursework spans computer networking, operating systems, and foundational information security. A dedicated course in network security introduced me to the fundamentals of defensive operations—including SIEM configuration, packet analysis using Wireshark, and identifying standard log anomalies—which I regularly put into practice through hands-on lab environments and capture-the-flag (CTF) challenges. Outside of core requirements, I gravitate toward electives in ethical hacking and digital forensics, where I get to think critically not just about how a network functions, but how an adversary might exploit it and how to effectively harden it.
